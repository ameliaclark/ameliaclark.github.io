PROTESTING WON'T CHANGE THE WORLD BUT YOU CAN


Being non-violent is the best way to bring about change

Protests That Make Change

1. Bring a group of people together

2. Make a plan and agenda

3. Spread the word

4. Get creative with your message, make your posters stand out and make them memorable

5. Gain the support of influencers

6. Invite media to come and make sure to document it for later promotion

7. Keep the message going after the protest
